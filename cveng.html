Music and Teaching

Since 2010 I have been playing and recording for lots of bands in several venues, studios, teathers and arenas in Italy, France, UK, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Slovenia and Argentina.
You can listen and watch on my website at bit.ly/leog.

Currently working on an editorial project called "Atlante Musicale" (click bit.ly/atlantemusicalefor further informations)
and giving seminaries on how sound works trough natural harmonics and rhythm.

Since 2016 I have been teaching as music professor in public middle schools through the center of Italy.
In 2012, together with other collegues, I opened the music school in "Forte Prenestino", Rome.
Since 2008 I have been teaching music privately and in several music schools.
