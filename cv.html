CURRICULUM VITAE

Hard worker with propension to learn new languages. Years spent teaching in middle schools gave me the opportunity to deal with several different personalities at a time and manage to get through long term goals. I've always been very interested in computers and technology.

Music and Teaching

I have played and recorded in several venues, studios, teathers and arenas in Italy, France, UK, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Slovenia and Argentina. You can listen and watch on my website (bit.ly/leog).

Since 2016 I have been teaching as music professor in middle schools.

I am working on an editorial project called "Atlante Musicale" (bit.ly/atlantemusicale), currently available in Italian language;
I recently started giving seminaries about the nature of sound through singing natural harmonics and playing rhythms.

I have been teaching music since 2008.
